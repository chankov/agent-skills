Add the **Verification Contract** to the agent-hub orchestration flow, so a clearly stated requirement (e.g. "Retired/Disqualified behave like Walkover") cannot be silently dropped across a multi-agent run. The dispatcher now owns checkable acceptance assertions and refuses "done" until each is proven with evidence.

Added to the `using-agent-skills` discovery tree.
- **One orchestrator persona (breaking).** `orchestrator` and `orchestrator-careful` are consolidated into a single `orchestrator` that carries the careful (correctness-first) posture as its default and layers the Verification Contract on top â€” it builds the assertion list first, commissions a deep-researcher parity inventory, gates vertical micro-slices, requires runtime proof for UI/visibility/placement assertions, accepts only structured returns, and resets assertions on "wrong again". `orchestrator-careful` is **retired** (the pi-only persona roster drops from 14 to 13); its review-first behaviour is preserved in the surviving persona.
